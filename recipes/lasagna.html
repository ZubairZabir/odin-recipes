Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, a timeless culinary masterpiece, is a beloved dish that has its roots in Italy but has won hearts across the globe. This classic dish is a harmonious blend of rich, flavorful layers that combine to create a symphony of taste and texture. Each layer of lasagna is a testament to the art of cooking -silky sheets of pasta, alternating with a savory meat sauce, often a robust blend of ground beef and Italian herbs simmered to perfection. Nestled between these layers is the creamy richness of cheeses, typically a mix of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan, each adding its unique flavor and melt-in-the-mouth texture.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Red Wine
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Fresh Parsley
    Steps
  • Brown the Meat: Cook 1 lb of ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork) until no longer pink. Drain excess fat.
  • Add Aromatics: Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the meat. Cook until onion is translucent.
  • Make Meat Sauce: Stir in 28 oz of crushed tomatoes, 2 tbsp of tomato paste, 1 tbsp of Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  • Prepare Cheese Filling: Mix 15 oz of ricotta cheese, 1 egg, 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan, salt, pepper, and optional parsley.
  • Preheat Oven: Preheat to 375°F (190°C).
  • Layer 1: Spread thin meat sauce layer in a 9x13 inch dish.
  • Layer 2: Place lasagna noodles over sauce.
  • Layer 3: Spread ricotta mixture over noodles.
  • Layer 4: Sprinkle mozzarella over ricotta.
  • Repeat Layers: Repeat layers until all ingredients are used. Top with meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Cover with foil.
  • Bake: Bake covered for 25 mins, then uncovered for 25 mins until bubbly and browned.
  • Rest: Let sit for 10-15 mins before serving.
  • Serve: Cut into squares and enjoy with optional sides.
