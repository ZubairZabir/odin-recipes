Description

Indulge in the timeless delight of pizza, a culinary masterpiece that has captured hearts worldwide. Pizza is the ultimate comfort food, a savory symphony of flavors and textures that begins with a perfectly baked crust, crispy on the outside and delightfully chewy on the inside. It's then generously adorned with a vibrant tomato sauce, a canvas for an endless array of toppings that cater to every palate. Whether you are a fan of classic Margherita with its luscious mozzarella and fresh basil or prefer a bold combination of meats and veggies, pizza offers a slice of happiness for everyone. Share it with friends and family or savor it solo; there is no wrong way to enjoy this iconic dish.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose Flour
  • Warm Water
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Dried Oregano
  • Dried Basil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni SLices
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix flour, yeast, sugar, salt, olive oil, and warm water. Knead into a smooth dough and let it rise for 1-2 hours.
  2. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to its highest temperature (usually around 500°F or 260°C) with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  3. Roll Out Dough: Roll out the dough into a round or rectangular shape on a floured surface.
  4. Apply Sauce: Spread tomato sauce on the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  5. Add Toppings: Add shredded mozzarella and your choice of toppings.
  6. Bake: Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (use a pizza peel or the back of a baking sheet). Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Finish with Basil: Remove from the oven, top with fresh basil leaves, and optionally sprinkle with red pepper flakes and grated Parmesan.
  8. Slice and Serve: Let it cool slightly, then slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!
